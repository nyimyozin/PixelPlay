    How to Play Hangman

    Hangman is a word-guessing game where your goal is to guess a hidden word one letter at a time. Here's how to play:

    1. Objective: Your goal is to guess the word before you run out of allowed incorrect guesses.
    2. Game Interface: When you load the game, you'll see a UI with several key elements.
      • The Hangman Image: This image represents how many incorrect guesses you've made.
      • The Word Display: This area shows you the letters of the word you need to guess, initially as blank spaces.
      • The Hint: A hint is provided to help you guess the word.
      • Incorrect Guesses Counter: This displays how many incorrect guesses you've made out of the maximum allowed.
    3. Guessing Letters: To make a guess, click on the buttons below the game interface. These buttons represent the alphabet. Click on a letter you think is in the hidden word.
    4. Correct Guesses: If your guess is correct, the game will reveal the letter in the word display. If that letter appears multiple times in the word, all occurrences will be revealed.
    5. Incorrect Guesses: If your guess is wrong, the hangman image will start to take shape, and the incorrect guesses counter will increase.
    6. Winning: You win the game if you correctly guess all the letters in the word before you reach the maximum allowed incorrect guesses. In this game, the maximum allowed incorrect guesses are set to 6.
    7. Losing: If you reach the maximum allowed incorrect guesses before guessing the word, you lose the game.
    8. Play Again: After you win or lose, you can hit the "Play Again" button to start a new round with a new word.

    That's the basics of how to play Hangman. It's a classic word-guessing game, and the challenge is to figure out the word with as few incorrect guesses as possible. Have fun playing!
